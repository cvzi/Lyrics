<p><i>Lyrics</i> scrapes songtexts from the Genius.com website when you play a song in your audio player. Only the lyrics are shown, no other information, annotations, comments, etc. from Genius.com. You can open the Genius.com page of the song from the hamburger menu to quickly view the regular Genius.com page of the currently playing song.<br><br>In case lyrics don't show up even though you are playing a song in your audio player, you can press the button <i>Enable Notification Access</i> to give the app access to all your notifications. The app then tries to extract the song title and artist from the audio player's notification. Known apps that require notification access: <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/org.schabi.newpipe/">NewPipe</a></p>
